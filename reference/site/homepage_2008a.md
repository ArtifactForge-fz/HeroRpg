Posted by Eden on Tuesday, March 11th, 2008, 20:08:45 PM To the very few players that it may concern: After much (ok, not much, just a day) of contemplation, I have decided to put Hero on a hiatus. This doesn't mean it will never be finished, just not in the near future. There clearly has not been enough interest in seeing it completed, so there shouldn't be much concern if I decide to take a break from developing it. While designing the faction/pvp system for Hero, I realized the scope of what I wanted to code was far too large to implement in Hero and have it remain a pure RPG. For this reason I have decide to code it as a new game. Yes, you heard right, *another* new game. The new game will not be an RPG, at least not in the full sense of the word. It will contain many RPG elements, but it will be moving away from focusing on single characters. It will be a casual game that can be either strategic or non-strategic and can be played solo (much like Hero) or with other players. It will include player-versus-player interaction from the beginning and will borrow many features in the single-player aspect from Hero and Legend of Arcadia. The multiplayer aspect will be fully integrated with the single-player game (though you never have to play alone if you don't want to) and will be based on diplomacy, commerce, and exploration. I won't go into the full details of the game until much later; I'll keep our (few) readers posted on the game's progress. As soon as the wiki for the game goes live, a link will be posted here. It could be a few weeks or a few months. v3 Open Testing Posted by Eden on Monday, January 28th, 2008, 02:15:39 AM Version 3.0 is nearing completion but is not yet ready for release. Instead of holding the current progress until release, we've decided to let everyone test what we have so far. If you're interested in participating, go to http://dev.herorpg.net/ and register. Keep in mind this IS a testing environment and your characters WILL be wiped before the final release. Maybe even multiple times. However, we feel that open testing will expedite the development process. If you are interested in helping, please, please, leave us feedback on the forum. Tell us what we can do to improve the existing game, or what we need to fix. Your input will be greatly appreciated. Version 3.0 Posted by Eden on Friday, December 28th, 2007, 00:52:15 AM For those that do not know - Version 3.0 is in the making and will soon be released. Due to the nature of the update, all characters and content will be wiped completely. We will be redesigning the world from the ground up, including races, locations, monsters, items, and quests. The most major changes in the new version are the new skill system and the Eidolon system, both of which will play a large role in character development. You can read about the upcoming update in the manual under Version 3.0. A link to it is on the front page.
