Human - HeroRPG Manual Human From HeroRPG Manual Jump to: navigation , search Humans were the first race to populate the world. They are a taller race whose appearance differs greatly from person to person. Their homeland is found on the plains of Averast where their kingdom of Eldor flourishes. Humans have their own unique endeavors that other races don't care to bother with. They have become obsessed with trade and making their economy thrive between their cities.
