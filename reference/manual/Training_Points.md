Training Points - HeroRPG Manual Training Points From HeroRPG Manual Jump to: navigation , search Training Points are acquired by two methods: Either leveling up, in which case you will receive 2 Training Points, or by completing certain quests, from which you may receive a certain amount of Training Points depending on the quest. Note that not all quests reward Training Points. You may check how many Training Points you currently have by visiting the Academy .
