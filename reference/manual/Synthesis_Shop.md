Synthesis Shop - HeroRPG Manual Synthesis Shop From HeroRPG Manual Jump to: navigation , search The Synthesis Shop is a special shop located in some cities (you'll find it below the Shop link). This shop differs from the normal shop in that you exchange both Platinum and/or Gold as well as items for new items. The resulting item will often have greater stats than the items used to craft it. The process is known as 'synthesizing' because it requires one or more items to be combined ("synthesized") into a resulting item.
