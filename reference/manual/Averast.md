Averast - HeroRPG Manual Averast From HeroRPG Manual Jump to: navigation , search Averast is a landmass on the continent of Van Arius in the world of Exos . It is located to the west of the Sea of Stars and is the third largest region . It is home to the Human and Arkan races and contains two major cities, the Royal City of Eldor and Saratus . The lands of Averast are mostly flat and plain with rolling hills. The northern border is a natural mountainous barrier that Humans and Arkan have never crossed. The eastern border is the Sea of Stars that no sailors have ever sailed across and returned home. The southern border is the Ocean of Asterius . The western border is an inland sea that contains the isle of Juneros which contains small human settlements.
