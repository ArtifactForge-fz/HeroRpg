Techs - HeroRPG Manual Techs From HeroRPG Manual Jump to: navigation , search Techs have many uses within the game, including healing, stat increase, and attack. Techs are purchased at the Academy using Training Points . You can see the effect of a Tech by double clicking the icon in either the Academy or Tech windows. To equip a Tech, click the Techs link and drag the Tech icons into your Set Boxes. You may have up to 24 Techs ready to use at any time, separated into three groups of eight. To use a Tech in battle, simply click the icon.
